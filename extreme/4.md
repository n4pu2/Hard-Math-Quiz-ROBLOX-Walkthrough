
$$i^2 = -1$$

Define $z = a +bi$.

Therefore $\bar{z} = a - bi$ and $|z| = \sqrt{ a^2 + b^2 }$.

$$
\begin{align}
\bar{z} + i|z| &= 12 +9i \\
(a - bi) + i(\sqrt{ a^2+b^2 }) &= 12 + 9i \\
a -bi + i\sqrt{ a^2+b^2 } &= 12 + 9i \\
a + (-b + \sqrt{ a^2 + b^2 })i &= 12 + 9i
\end{align}
$$


$$
\begin{align}
\mathrm{Re}(a + (-b + \sqrt{ a^2 + b^2 })i) &= \mathrm{Re}(12 + 9i) \\
a &= 12
\end{align}
$$

$$
\begin{align}
\mathrm{Im}(a + (-b + \sqrt{ a^2 + b^2 })i) &= \mathrm{Im}(12 + 9i) \\
-b + \sqrt{ a^2 + b^2 } &= 9 \\
-b + \sqrt{ 12^2 + b^2 } &= 9 \\
\sqrt{ 144 + b^2 } &= 9 + b \\
144 + b^2 &= 81 + 18b + b^2 \\
63 + b^2 &= 18b+b^2 \\
63 &= 18b  \\
b &= \frac{63}{18} \\
b &= \frac{7}{2} \\
b &= 3.5
\end{align}
$$

Therefore $\mathrm{Im}(z) = b = 3.5$
