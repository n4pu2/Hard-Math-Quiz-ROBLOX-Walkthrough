Define $m = 3^x$ 

$$
\begin{align}
7m + m + m &= 9^x \cdot 27m \cdot 9m \\
9m &= (3^2)^x \cdot 3^3m \cdot 3^2m \\
9m &= (3^x)^2 \cdot 3^3m \cdot 3^2m \\
9m &= m^2 \cdot 3^3m \cdot 3^2m \\
3^2m &= 3^5m^4 \\
1 &= 3^3m^3 \\
1^3 &= (3m)^3 \\
3m &= 1 \\
m &= \frac{1}{3} \\
\end{align}
$$

If $m = \frac{1}{3}$ then $3^x = \frac{1}{3}$ :

$$
\begin{align}
3^x &= \frac{1}{3} \\
3^x &= 3^{-1} \\
x &= -1
\end{align}
$$
